Our Story

Square Transport started when we noticed that delivery trucks cause traffic jams and pollution in our cities. We thought there had to be a better way.

Started in Stockholm in 2022, we're a group of university students studying engineering and computer science. We're learning how to build robots and AI systems while working on this project.

We're excited to be part of changing how delivery works, combining what we're learning in school with real-world problem solving.
