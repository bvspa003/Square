Development Roadmap

2025

Design & Development

Complete robot design, AI development, and initial prototype testing. Focus on perfecting the vision-only navigation system and modular design.

Early 2026

Regulatory Approval & Pilots

Obtain necessary regulatory approvals and launch pilot programs with select partners in controlled urban environments.

Mid 2026

Commercial Launch

Begin commercial operations in Stockholm and other Swedish cities, establishing key partnerships with retailers and logistics companies.

2027

National Expansion

Scale operations across Sweden and begin expansion to other Nordic countries. Introduce additional robot variants for specialized delivery needs.

2029

International Rollout

Launch operations in European markets and explore opportunities in North America and Asia. Establish global partnerships and manufacturing.
