Our Story

Square Transport was born from a simple observation: last-mile delivery in urban environments was broken. Traffic congestion, parking challenges, and environmental concerns were making traditional delivery methods increasingly unsustainable.

Founded in Stockholm in 2022, we assembled a team of world-class engineers and designers from companies like Spotify, Ericsson, and Volvo. Our diverse backgrounds in AI, robotics, and sustainable technology convergered around a shared vision: autonomous delivery robots that would make cities cleaner, quieter, and more efficient.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the autonomous delivery revolution, combining Swedish design principles with cutting-edge AI technology to create solutions that benefit businesses, consumers, and our environment.
